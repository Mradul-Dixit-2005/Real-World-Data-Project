# Real World Data Project - Retail Sales Analysis

## Objective

Analyze retail sales data to identify trends and business insights.

## Domain

Retail

## Dataset

Monthly Sales Dataset

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## Analysis Performed

* Statistical Summary
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Monthly Sales Comparison
* Business Insights

## Key Findings

* Sales increased steadily throughout the year.
* December recorded the highest sales.
* Year-end months generated maximum revenue.
* Positive growth trend observed.

## Conclusion

The retail business demonstrated consistent growth with peak performance during the holiday season.
